About me

I’m Aidan, a third year undergraduate at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign studying computer science and statistics. I’m originally from the Chicagoland area, I’ve worked in multiple research groups on campus, and I’ve previously done internships at AARP and Amazon. I’m mainly interested in working with distributed systems, video, or computer vision, however I’m open to most software engineering roles. I’ll be looking for new grad software engineer roles once I graduate in May 2023.

While I’ve already completed all of my undergrad degree requirements, I am not planning on graduating until May 2023 and am currently taking a hiatus from my computer science coursework. After my fourth semester (spring 2021), I started to feel pretty burnt out from school. So, I decided to shift my focus for the entirety of 2022 away from schoolwork and towards trying to figure out how I want to spend my future. I landed two internships at Google and Twitch, for the winter and summer, respectively, and am going to be studying abroad in the fall, which I think all these experiences will help kick me out of feeling burnt out. I truly think I’m doing this for the best of maximizing my learning, albeit in a non-academic environment. I’m planning on somewhat resuming my computer science coursework while abroad, and taking some classes I’m interested in the spring 2023 semester.

Outside of my school and work life, I’m a coffee purist, a freak who spends too much money on his mechanical keyboard, and a full-time plant killer. I also really enjoy playing chess and rowing, and I’ve been driving a 1995 Toyota Corolla since I’ve learned to drive in 2016.

You can best reach out to me either on LinkedIn or Instagram.
